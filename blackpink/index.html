BLACKPINK

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment since 2016.
The group is composed of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa.
Record labels: YG Entertainment, Interscope Records, Polydor Records, YGEX, Universal Music Japan

Lets meet up the four talented members of BLACKPINK!

Jennie

Jennie is a rapper and vocalist of BLACKPINK. She's known for her charisma and strong stage presence.


Lisa

Lisa is the main dancer and lead rapper. Her powerful dance moves and style captivate the audience.


Jisoo

Jisoo is the lead vocalist and visual of the group. She is adored for her beauty and sweet vocals.


Rosé

Rosé is the main vocalist and guitarist. Her unique voice and musical talent are highly praised.
